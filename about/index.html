SW Sam Woods Founder, BarrelCompare · Based in Hertfordshire About the Founder — Sam Woods Barrel Compare was born from lived experience, community conversations, and a deep belief that people deserve better. Growing up in a Caribbean household, sending a barrel "home" was never just about shipping items. It was about love, support, sacrifice, and staying connected to family, culture, and community. It was discussed everywhere: at family gatherings, in local shops, and over the phone with relatives abroad alongside the same questions: Who's reliable? Who's affordable? Who can actually be trusted? For too long, people have relied on word of mouth, guesswork, and inconsistent experiences when choosing who to ship with. A lack of transparency within the process has often left customers frustrated, confused, and out of pocket when expectations are unclear or communication breaks down. But the reality is, trusted shipping companies face these frustrations too. Good freight forwarders and barrel shippers work hard to build strong reputations, provide reliable services, and create positive customer experiences that lead to repeat business. Yet there has never really been one central place where they can clearly showcase their services, explain their process, communicate expectations, and build trust with the communities they serve. That is where Barrel Compare comes in. After watching countless barrels being packed and shipped over the years — from helping family send essentials to Grenada to supporting church donations and care packages for loved ones — I realised the community deserved a clearer, more transparent way to navigate the process. Barrel Compare was created to bring customers and shippers into the same space with greater clarity, accountability, and collaboration. The aim is simple: to remove confusion, reduce friction, and help people make informed decisions with confidence. Customers can compare services, understand typical costs and delivery expectations, and learn from real community experiences. At the same time, shipping companies have the opportunity to present their services transparently, communicate clearly, and demonstrate the professionalism and care behind their business. Because transparency benefits everyone. For customers, it creates confidence. For shipping companies, it builds trust, strengthens relationships, and supports long-term loyalty. This platform is about more than comparing prices. It is about improving standards across the industry, protecting people's hard-earned money, and creating a more open, informed, and accountable experience for everyone involved. At its heart, Barrel Compare is rooted in service and community. As the platform grows, a percentage of profits will be donated to charities supporting both the UK Caribbean community and the countries where barrels are sent. Because for many of us, sending a barrel home has always been about helping family, supporting communities, and giving back. I see Barrel Compare as a digital meeting place for the community — a space where customers can share experiences, where trusted businesses can stand proudly behind their service, and where better standards can be built together. A platform built by the community, for the community. Because it is time our community was served better. We need you.
