Most UK shippers use this as their everyday option. 55 US gallons · Most common · Everyday shipping option Large Barrel ? Despite the name, the Large Barrel holds less — 44 Imperial gallons. 'Large' refers to its build quality, not its size. These barrels are heavier-duty and reinforced to handle the pressure of being stacked in shipping containers without bursting.
