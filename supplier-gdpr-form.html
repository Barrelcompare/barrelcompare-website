Supplier Data Agreement

Complete and sign this agreement before receiving customer quote requests from BarrelCompare.

Why this is required: Under UK GDPR (UK General Data Protection Regulation), BarrelCompare must have a signed Data Processing Agreement in place with every supplier before sharing customer personal data. This includes names, email addresses, phone numbers, and postcodes submitted via quote requests. You will not receive customer contact details until this agreement is signed and confirmed by BarrelCompare.

1. Purpose of data sharing

BarrelCompare will share customer personal data (name, email address, phone number, UK postcode, destination, and barrel details) solely for the purpose of responding to a customer's quote request. Data must not be used for any other purpose.

2. Your obligations as data processor

You agree to: process customer data only on documented instructions from BarrelCompare; keep data confidential and not disclose it to third parties; delete or return all customer data within 30 days of the quote process being completed or abandoned; implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect the data.

3. No marketing use

Customer data shared via BarrelCompare quote requests must not be added to any marketing list, newsletter, or CRM system for the purpose of future marketing communications unless the customer has given separate, explicit consent directly to you.

4. Data breach notification

In the event of a data breach involving customer data received via BarrelCompare, you must notify BarrelCompare at hello@barrelcompare.co.uk within 24 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

5. Sub-processing

You must not pass customer data to any third party or sub-processor without prior written consent from BarrelCompare.

6. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Both parties agree to comply with all applicable UK data protection legislation including UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.
