What do these terms mean?
Door-to-doorSupplier collects from your UK address and delivers to the recipient's address overseas.
Drop-offYou take the barrel to the supplier's UK depot. Recipient collects from the overseas port.
UK pickupSupplier collects from your UK address. Recipient collects from the overseas port.
DistributionSupplier handles onward delivery from the overseas port to the final address.

Why some prices say "contact for pricing"

We recommend choosing a supplier where possible whose pricing has been confirmed. Suppliers whose pricing has been confirmed have had their credentials and customer track record independently confirmed by BarrelCompare. Others are in our queue — we are checking their details before we can display confirmed pricing.
