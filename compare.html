What do these terms mean?
Door-to-door Supplier collects from your UK address and delivers to the recipient's address overseas.
Drop-off You take the barrel to the supplier's UK depot. Recipient collects from the overseas port.
UK pickup Supplier collects from your UK address. Recipient collects from the overseas port.
Distribution Supplier handles onward delivery from the overseas port to the final address.
⚠️ Customs note: Customs restrictions may apply depending on your destination. Destination-specific guidance will be added in a future update.

Why some prices say "contact for pricing"

We recommend choosing a Verified supplier where possible. Verified suppliers have had their pricing, credentials and customer track record independently confirmed by BarrelCompare. Unverified suppliers are in our queue — we are checking their details before we can display confirmed pricing.
