Community & giving back

Suggest a Charity

Charity begins at home — and that's what we propose to do here.

Why we're doing this

BarrelCompare was built for the Caribbean community in the UK. Many of us send barrels home not just as a practical act, but as an act of love — support for family, for home, for community.

We want to extend that spirit further. We are looking to identify charities and organisations in the Caribbean that are doing genuinely good work — whether in education, health, disaster relief, youth development, or community support — and who would benefit from recognition, fundraising, or practical support.

If you know of an organisation making a real difference, tell us about them. We'll review every suggestion and may feature them as part of a future fundraising or awareness initiative.
