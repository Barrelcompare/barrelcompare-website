How it works

Three steps. No surprises.

We do the digging so you don't have to. Just compare, choose, and ship with confidence.

1

Choose your destination

Tell us where you're shipping to — Jamaica, Trinidad, Guyana, Ghana or Nigeria. We'll show you every verified UK shipper covering that route.
