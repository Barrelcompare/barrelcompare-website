Barrel sizes

Which barrel do you need?

Standard Barrel ? The Standard Barrel is actually the bigger of the two — 55 US gallons. It's called 'Standard' because it's the most widely produced barrel size in the world. Most UK shippers use this as their everyday option.

55 US gallons · Most common · Everyday shipping option

Large Barrel ? Despite the name, the Large Barrel holds less — 44 Imperial gallons. 'Large' refers to its build quality, not its size. These barrels are heavier-duty and reinforced to handle the pressure of being stacked in shipping containers without bursting. They're the premium option.

44 Imperial gallons · Heavy-duty · Reinforced for stacking
