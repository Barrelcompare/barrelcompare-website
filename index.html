Common questions

Questions people ask before they ship.

Quick answers to the most common questions. See the full FAQ for more.

How does BarrelCompare make money?

We may receive a small fee when you request a quote through us — but this never influences ranking, listing position or what we tell you about a supplier. Suppliers cannot pay for better placement.

Are the prices guaranteed?

Prices shown are starting points and based on standard barrel size, weight, and route. Final pricing comes directly from the supplier you choose, based on your specific shipment.

How long does shipping usually take?

Caribbean routes typically take 5–7 weeks door-to-door, depending on supplier, sailing schedule, and current port conditions. Check the live alerts at the top of every page for up-to-date delays.

What can I send in a barrel?

Most personal items, clothing, non-perishable food, household goods and small gifts. Each supplier has slightly different rules — always check the prohibited items list before packing.
