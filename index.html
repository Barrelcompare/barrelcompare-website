Choose the right size for your shipment. All weights are approximate, confirm with your supplier.

Standard barrel

Approx. 85–90cm tall · 55–57cm wide

The most common option used by UK shipping companies. Lighter than steel barrels and easier to handle. Many people reuse them at the destination for storage, including water storage.

Best for: Clothing, food, toiletries, household goods, children's items
Large barrel
Image
[Replace me]

Large barrel

Approx. 100–107cm tall · 57–60cm wide

Offers extra space for larger shipments. Some shipping companies charge a higher rate for this size. Not all shippers offer large barrels, so always check before booking.

Best for: Larger loads, extra clothing, bulkier household items
